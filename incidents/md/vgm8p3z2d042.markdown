## Postmortem

*Posted Sep 11, 2024 - 13:53 UTC*

We observed an elevated error rate of approximately 0.2% affecting .page domains. The root cause was traced to latency issues with one of our third-party service providers.

**Root Cause:**

The issue was caused by latency problems from one of our service providers, which affected the performance of our systems handling .page domains.

**Mitigation:**

Upon identifying the issue, we quickly migrated the affected sites to an alternative provider. This action immediately reduced the error rate.

## Resolved

*Posted Jun 19, 2024 - 18:02 UTC*

This incident has been resolved.
