## Postmortem

*Posted Jun 29, 2022 - 15:29 UTC*

The underlying reason for the increased error rates on media delivery was a partial outage of Fastly’s Image Optimizer: [https://status.fastly.com/incidents/s542qjby04vb](https://status.fastly.com/incidents/s542qjby04vb)

We have opened an issue with Fastly support, asking for an official statement about how Fastly is going to address this issue and prevent future such outages.

## Resolved

*Posted Jun 29, 2022 - 04:00 UTC*

From 01:03 UTC to 04:04 UTC we saw increased error rates for media requests.
