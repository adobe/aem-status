Executive Summary

On February 7, 2026 between 01:57 (UTC) and 02:34 (UTC), we observed an increase of errors on requests served from Cloudflare backends. As a result the delivery service was impacted with a error rate of ~0.035%. Our team detected the issue within 35 minutes through automated monitoring and initiated failover to the alternate stack within 2 minutes. The delivery service was restored by 02:34 UTC. Some customers experienced an increase of 413 responses after that until we switched them back to Cloudflare backends at Feburary 9, 2026 16:30 (UTC).

Root Cause

An underlying issue with Cloudflare's R2 service caused the increase of errors.

Resolution

The issue was resolved by switching projects using Cloudflare backends to another cloud provider at 02:33 UTC. After the resoluiton of the underlying issue at 04:43 UTC, we switched the affected projects back to Cloudflare backends on Feburary 9, 2026 16:30 (UTC). In that time frame some customers experienced an increase of 413 responses for very large indexes.

Action Items
