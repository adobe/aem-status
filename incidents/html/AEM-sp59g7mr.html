Cloudflare Outage with Minor Impact on Delivery

Postmortem

Executive Summary

On November 18, 2025, between 11:30 UTC and 14:40 UTC, a global Cloudflare outage impacted our delivery services with an overall error rate of 0.08%. Our team detected the issue within minutes through automated monitoring and initiated failover to AWS within 11 minutes. The delivery service experienced minimal impact and was fully restored by 14:40 UTC.

The root cause was a global outage at Cloudflare, lasting from 11:20 UTC to 17:06 UTC.

The impact on the delivery service was barely noticeable at an overall error rate of 0.08%, from 11:30 UTC to 14:40 UTC. The vast majority of projects were only impacted for 12 minutes until 11:42 UTC, and less than 1% of projects experienced elevated errors until 14:40 UTC.

What went well

  • Automated monitoring detected the issue within minutes
  • Failover to AWS was executed within 11 minutes
  • Multi-cloud strategy limited customer impact

Action items

  1. In case of a future Cloudflare outage, we always need to switch to the healthy stack at the DNS level from the beginning
  2. Improve runbook to provide clearer steps and better help us manage a global Cloudflare outage
  3. Clearly define all technical services that belong to the delivery and publishing business service SLOs
  4. Consolidate logs from all relevant technical services to help the on-call engineer with an overview during an outage, as well as facilitate the RCA
