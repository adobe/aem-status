Cloudflare Outage with Minor Impact on Authoring and Publishing

Postmortem

Executive Summary

On November 18, 2025, between 11:31 UTC and 15:30 UTC, a global Cloudflare outage impacted our publishing services with an overall error rate of 0.61%. Our team detected the issue within minutes through automated monitoring and initiated failover to AWS within 11 minutes. The delivery service experienced minimal impact (0.07% error rate) and was fully restored by 14:40 UTC. The publishing service was restored by 15:30 UTC. No data loss occurred, and the vast majority of customers experienced either no impact or brief interruptions.

The root cause was a global outage at Cloudflare, lasting from 11:20 UTC to 17:06 UTC.

Action items

  1. In case of a future Cloudflare outage, we always need to switch to the healthy stack at the DNS level from the beginning
  2. Improve runbook to provide clearer steps and better help us manage a global Cloudflare outage
  3. Clearly define all technical services that belong to the delivery and publishing business service SLOs
  4. Consolidate logs from all relevant technical services to help the on-call engineer with an overview during an outage, as well as facilitate the RCA
